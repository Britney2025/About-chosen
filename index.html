About me

My name is Britnie Atieno. I am currently a student at Zindua School, trying to explore new opportunities in education while growing my creative side. I am a crochet artist and I run a small crochet brand, Looped by Chosen. As a passionate crocheter, I love creating handmade pieces that reflect style and creativity.

As an introvert, I'm on a journey to self-discovery - learning to express myself, find my voice and build confidence. I'm learning to grow both personally and professionally.
